 # Privacy Policy for Difela 4Angie

  Last updated: March 23, 2026

  Difela 4Angie is an offline hymnal app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app
  handles and how that information is used.

  ## Information We Collect

  Difela 4Angie does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal
  information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

  The app stores the following information locally on your device to support core features:

  - Your selected hymnbook
  - Your liked or favorite hymns
  - Your recently viewed hymns

  This information is stored only on your device using local app storage.

  ## How We Use Information

  The locally stored information is used only to:

  - Remember your hymnbook selection
  - Show your favorites
  - Show your recently opened hymns
  - Improve your experience inside the app

  ## Data Sharing

  Difela 4Angie does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

  Based on the current version of the app, the app does not send your favorites or recently viewed
  hymns to an external server.

  ## Data Retention

  The app keeps locally stored preferences and hymn activity on your device until:

  - You clear the app's data
  - You uninstall the app
  - A future version of the app changes storage behavior

  ## Children's Privacy

  Difela 4Angie does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

  ## Security

  Because the app stores limited preference data locally on your device, protecting your device and
  its access controls helps protect that information.

  No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but the app is designed to minimize data
  handling.

  ## Third-Party Services

  Based on the current version of the app, Difela 4Angie does not require account login, does not
  include ads, and does not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

  If a future version adds third-party services, this Privacy Policy should be updated before
  release.

  ## External Links

  The app may open external links, such as a Facebook page, using your device's browser or
  installed apps. Those external services are governed by their own privacy policies.

  ## Your Choices

  You can stop all data storage by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's local data through
  your device settings.

  ## Changes to This Privacy Policy

  This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates should be posted at the same
  public URL used for the app's Play Store listing.
